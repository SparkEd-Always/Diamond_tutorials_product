Introduction

Welcome to Sparky, a tutorial and school management application developed by SparkEd ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Sparky ("the App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Contact Information

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use the App:

For All Users:
  • Phone Number: Required for account creation and authentication via OTP
  • Name: Your full name for identification within the app
  • User Type: Whether you are a teacher, parent, or admin
For Teachers:
  • Teacher Information: Name, phone number, assigned classes
  • Teaching Schedule: Classes and subjects taught
For Parents:
  • Parent Information: Name, phone number
  • Student Relationship: Information about your children enrolled in the tutorial
For Students (provided by parents/admins):
  • Student Information: Name, class, roll number
  • Attendance Records: Daily attendance status (present/absent)
  • Academic Information: Class, section, enrollment details

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information:

Device Information:
  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system and version (Android)
  • Device identifiers (Firebase Cloud Messaging token)
  • App version
Usage Information:
  • Features accessed within the app
  • Screens viewed and time spent
  • Interactions with notifications
  • Error logs and crash reports
Network Information:
  • IP address
  • Internet service provider
  • Connection type (Wi-Fi, mobile data)

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Firebase (Google):
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for push notifications
  • Firebase Analytics for app usage statistics
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting

For more information on how Firebase handles data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Authentication: Verify your identity using phone number and OTP
  • Account Management: Create and manage your user account
  • Attendance Tracking: Record and display student attendance
  • Communication: Enable messaging between teachers and parents
  • Notifications: Send push notifications about attendance, messages, and announcements
  • Data Display: Show relevant information based on your role (teacher/parent)

2.2 App Improvement

  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the app
  • Error Detection: Identify and fix bugs and crashes
  • Feature Development: Develop new features based on usage patterns
  • Performance Optimization: Improve app speed and reliability

2.3 Communication

  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Updates: Notify you about app updates and new features
  • Announcements: Send important notifications about the tutorial/school

2.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 Within the Tutorial/School

Information is shared within your tutorial or school as follows:

Teachers can see:
  • Student names and attendance records for their assigned classes
  • Messages from parents in their classes
  • School announcements
Parents can see:
  • Their children's attendance records
  • Messages from teachers
  • School announcements
  • Fee information for their children
Admins can see:
  • All teacher, parent, and student information within their school
  • Complete attendance records
  • All messages and communications
  • System-wide statistics and reports

3.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

Hosting Provider:
  • Railway.app: Hosts our backend servers and database
  • Purpose: Store and process app data
  • Data Shared: All user data stored in the app
  • Location: Cloud servers (location may vary)
Notification Services:
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Delivers push notifications
  • Purpose: Send attendance alerts and messages
  • Data Shared: Device tokens, notification content
  • Provider: Google
Analytics Services:
  • Firebase Analytics: App usage statistics
  • Purpose: Understand user behavior and improve the app
  • Data Shared: Anonymized usage data, device info
  • Provider: Google

3.3 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Important: We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to:

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government requests
  • Protection of our rights and property
  • Investigation of fraud or security issues

4. Data Security

4.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards:
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Encrypted Storage: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest on our servers
  • Secure Authentication: Phone number verification via OTP (no passwords to store)
  • Access Controls: Role-based access ensures users only see authorized information
  • Regular Backups: Daily backups to prevent data loss
Organizational Safeguards:
  • Limited access to personal data by employees
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Incident response procedures

4.2 Security Limitations

No system is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for:

  • Keeping your phone and OTP secure
  • Not sharing your account with others
  • Reporting any suspected security breaches to support@sparked.in

5. Your Data Rights

5.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal information stored in the app
  • Correct: Update incorrect or outdated information
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

How to Exercise: Contact support@sparked.in or use the in-app settings.

5.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information.

How to Request Deletion:

  • Email support@sparked.in with subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Provide your phone number and user type
  • We will process your request within 30 days

What Gets Deleted:

  • Your account and profile information
  • Your messages and communications
  • Your device tokens

What May Be Retained:

  • Attendance records (required by schools for legal/educational purposes)
  • Anonymized analytics data
  • Data required for legal compliance (e.g., financial records)

5.3 Opt-Out of Notifications

You can control notifications:

  • In-App: Settings → Notifications
  • Device Level: Android Settings → Apps → Sparky → Notifications
  • Complete Opt-Out: Disable all notifications in app settings

Note: Disabling notifications may impact your ability to receive important updates about attendance and messages.

6. Children's Privacy

6.1 Age Requirements

Sparky is designed for educational purposes and may be used by minors under parental supervision.

User Age Groups:

  • Teachers/Admins: Must be 18+ years old
  • Parents: Must be 18+ years old
  • Students: May be of any school age, but accounts are managed by parents/admins

6.2 Parental Consent

  • Parents create accounts and provide student information
  • Parents have full control over their children's data
  • Parents can request deletion of their children's data at any time

6.3 Data About Children

We collect the following information about students:

  • Name, class, and roll number
  • Attendance records
  • Communication between teachers and parents

This information is only accessible to:

  • The student's parents
  • Teachers assigned to the student's class
  • School administrators

7. Data Retention

7.1 Retention Periods

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services:

Active Users:
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Attendance records: Retained for the current academic year + 3 years (educational requirement)
  • Messages: Retained for 2 years
Inactive Users:
  • Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted
  • You will receive email notification before deletion
Deleted Accounts:
  • Most data deleted within 30 days of deletion request
  • Some data retained for legal compliance (e.g., financial records)

7.2 Backup Retention

  • Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days after deletion
  • Backups are securely stored and not accessible for general use
