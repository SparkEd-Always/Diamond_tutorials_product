# Notices & Announcements Requirements

## User Story
As a school administrator/communication officer, I want to efficiently create, distribute, and manage official notices and announcements using automated multi-channel distribution so that important information reaches the intended audience timely and effectively while maintaining proper records and compliance.

## Actors
- **School Administrator** → Creates official notices, manages distribution lists, ensures compliance
- **Communication Officer** → Coordinates messaging, manages channels, tracks engagement
- **Principal** → Approves critical announcements, authorizes policy communications
- **Department Heads** → Submit departmental notices, review relevant announcements
- **Students & Parents** → Receive notices, acknowledge important announcements, provide feedback
- **Alumni & Community** → Receive institutional updates, participate in events, engage with school

## Journey (Steps)

### 1. Notice Creation & Content Management
- Draft notices with appropriate formatting and official branding
- Add attachments, images, and multimedia content as needed
- Set priority levels and categorize notices by type and audience
- Route notices for approval based on content and importance

### 2. Audience Segmentation & Targeting
- Define target audiences based on roles, classes, departments, or criteria
- Create distribution lists for different stakeholder groups
- Handle personalized notices and individual communications
- Manage opt-in/opt-out preferences for different communication types

### 3. Multi-channel Distribution
- Distribute via website, mobile app, email, SMS, and physical boards
- Schedule notices for optimal timing and maximum reach
- Handle urgent communications with priority delivery mechanisms
- Ensure accessibility compliance for diverse audience needs

### 4. Delivery Tracking & Confirmation
- Track delivery status across all communication channels
- Monitor read receipts and engagement metrics
- Follow up on critical notices requiring acknowledgment
- Handle failed deliveries and alternative communication methods

### 5. Archive & Compliance Management
- Maintain comprehensive archives of all official communications
- Ensure regulatory compliance for official notices and disclosures
- Provide search and retrieval capabilities for historical notices
- Generate reports for audit and compliance purposes

## Pain Points
- **Manual Distribution**: Time-consuming manual posting across multiple channels
- **Targeting Challenges**: Difficulty reaching specific audience segments effectively
- **Delivery Confirmation**: Lack of visibility into who received and read important notices
- **Version Control**: Managing multiple versions and updates to the same notice
- **Format Inconsistency**: Inconsistent formatting and branding across different channels
- **Archive Management**: Difficulty organizing and retrieving historical notices
- **Emergency Communications**: Slow distribution for urgent or crisis communications
- **Language Barriers**: Challenges communicating with diverse linguistic communities

## Opportunities
- **Unified Communication Platform**: Single system for creating and distributing all notices
- **Smart Segmentation**: AI-powered audience targeting based on relevance and preferences
- **Multi-language Support**: Automatic translation and localization capabilities
- **Rich Media Integration**: Support for videos, infographics, and interactive content
- **Real-time Analytics**: Comprehensive engagement and effectiveness tracking
- **Emergency Broadcast**: Rapid emergency communication with multiple fail-safes
- **Mobile-first Design**: Optimized mobile experience for on-the-go communication

## Inputs
- **Notice Content**: Text, images, attachments, multimedia content, formatting requirements
- **Audience Data**: Stakeholder contact information, preferences, segmentation criteria
- **Approval Workflows**: Authorization requirements, approval hierarchies, compliance checkpoints
- **Distribution Channels**: Website, mobile app, email servers, SMS gateways, physical locations
- **Scheduling Information**: Publication dates, expiry dates, reminder schedules
- **Brand Guidelines**: Official templates, logos, formatting standards, style guides
- **Compliance Requirements**: Legal disclaimers, regulatory notices, mandatory disclosures

## Outputs
- **Published Notices**: Formatted notices distributed across all selected channels
- **Delivery Reports**: Comprehensive delivery status and engagement analytics
- **Archive Records**: Searchable database of all historical communications
- **Compliance Documentation**: Audit trails and regulatory compliance reports
- **Engagement Analytics**: Read rates, click-through rates, and audience response metrics
- **Failed Delivery Reports**: Undelivered messages and alternative contact attempts
- **Performance Insights**: Communication effectiveness and optimization recommendations

- [ ] Notice creation supports rich media content with drag-and-drop interface
- [ ] Multi-channel distribution reaches 99%+ of intended recipients within target timeframe
- [ ] Audience segmentation enables precise targeting with 50+ filter criteria
- [ ] Emergency broadcast system delivers critical notices within 5 minutes to all channels
- [ ] Delivery tracking provides real-time status updates with 95%+ accuracy
- [ ] Mobile optimization ensures proper display across all device types and screen sizes
- [ ] Archive system enables full-text search across 10+ years of historical notices
- [ ] Compliance tracking maintains complete audit trail for all official communications
- [ ] Multi-language support provides automated translation for 5+ local languages
- [ ] Analytics dashboard shows comprehensive engagement metrics and trend analysis

## System Interactions
- **Student Information System**: Access student and parent contact information for targeted communications
- **Employee Management**: Reach staff members based on departments, roles, and preferences
- **Website Management**: Publish notices on school website and portal pages
- **Mobile Application**: Push notifications and in-app notice distribution
- **Email/SMS Services**: Automated email and text message delivery with tracking
- **Document Management**: Store notice attachments and multimedia content
- **Calendar Integration**: Coordinate notice timing with academic calendar and events
- **Analytics Engine**: Track engagement metrics and communication effectiveness
- **Social Media**: Cross-post notices to official social media channels

## Edge Cases
- **System Outages**: Backup communication methods during platform failures
- **Emergency Situations**: Crisis communication protocols with multiple redundancies
- **Network Disruptions**: Offline notice distribution and delayed synchronization
- **Contact Information Changes**: Handling outdated contact details and delivery failures
- **Large File Attachments**: Distribution of notices with substantial multimedia content
- **Legal Notices**: Special handling for legally required communications and disclosures
- **Multi-campus Coordination**: Synchronized communication across multiple school locations
- **Third-party Integration**: Notice distribution through external platforms and services
- **Accessibility Requirements**: Special formatting for visually impaired and disabled recipients
- **Privacy Concerns**: Handling sensitive information and confidential communications

## Priority/Frequency
- **Priority**: High (Critical for institutional communication and stakeholder engagement)
- **Frequency**:
  - Daily communications: Routine notices, announcements, and updates
  - Weekly planning: Communication calendar and content strategy
  - Emergency broadcasting: Immediate distribution for urgent situations
  - Seasonal campaigns: Admission notices, exam updates, event announcements
  - Compliance communications: Regulatory notices and mandatory disclosures
- **Peak Usage**: Academic year start, exam periods, admission seasons, emergency situations
- **Critical Periods**: Crisis situations, regulatory deadlines, major institutional announcements
