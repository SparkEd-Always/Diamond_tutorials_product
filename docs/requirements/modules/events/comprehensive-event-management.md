# Comprehensive Event Management Requirements

## User Story
As an event coordinator/activities director, I want to manage the complete event lifecycle from planning through execution to post-event analysis including coordination with teachers, student clubs, vendors, logistics management, communication, and feedback collection so that school events are successful, educational, and contribute to institutional goals.

## Actors
- **Event Coordinator** → Manages complete event lifecycle, coordinates stakeholders, ensures successful execution
- **Activities Director** → Oversees event strategy, approves major events, manages event portfolio
- **Principal** → Provides institutional support, authorizes budgets, represents school at events
- **Teachers** → Lead event committees, mentor students, integrate academic objectives, supervise activities
- **Student Club Leaders** → Organize peer participation, coordinate student activities, represent student interests
- **Volunteers** → Support event execution, assist with logistics, provide additional manpower
- **Administrative Staff** → Handle registrations, manage documentation, coordinate internal logistics
- **Vendors** → Provide catering, equipment, services, decorations, technical support
- **Performers** → Deliver entertainment, conduct cultural programs, provide artistic content
- **Guests** → Attend events, provide feedback, participate in activities, support school community
- **Media** → Cover events, provide publicity, document achievements, promote school image
- **Government Authorities** → Issue permits, ensure compliance, provide security clearance, inspect safety

## Journey (Steps)

### 1. Event Planning & Setup
- **Fixing Dates**: Coordinate with academic calendar and avoid conflicts
- **Budget Planning**: Allocate funds and secure sponsorships
- **Venue Selection**: Book appropriate facilities and obtain permissions
- **Resource Planning**: Identify equipment, personnel, and service needs

### 2. Coordination & Team Management
- **Teacher Coordination**: Assign roles and responsibilities to faculty
- **Student Club Integration**: Engage student organizations and leadership
- **Vendor Management**: Contract and coordinate external service providers
- **Volunteer Coordination**: Recruit and manage volunteer participation

### 3. Logistics & Infrastructure Management
- **Seating Arrangements**: Plan optimal seating and venue layout
- **Transportation**: Coordinate participant and guest transportation
- **Refreshments**: Plan catering and hospitality arrangements
- **Security & Safety**: Implement safety protocols and security measures
- **IT Setup**: Arrange audio-visual equipment and technical support

### 4. Communication & Promotion
- **Invitations**: Design and distribute invitations to parents and guests
- **Notices & Announcements**: Communicate event details to all stakeholders
- **Marketing**: Promote events through various channels and media
- **Reminders**: Send timely reminders and updates to participants

### 5. Event Execution & On-day Management
- **On-the-day Coordination**: Manage real-time event execution
- **Problem Resolution**: Handle issues and emergencies as they arise
- **Quality Control**: Monitor event quality and participant experience
- **Documentation**: Capture photos, videos, and event proceedings

### 6. Post-Event Management & Analysis
- **Feedback Collection**: Gather feedback from participants and stakeholders
- **Financial Settlement**: Process vendor payments and expense reconciliation
- **Media Coverage**: Coordinate with media and manage publicity
- **Impact Assessment**: Evaluate event success and learning outcomes
- **Documentation & Reporting**: Compile comprehensive event reports

## Pain Points
- **Resource Coordination**: Managing multiple resources and vendors simultaneously
- **Timeline Management**: Coordinating complex timelines with many dependencies
- **Budget Control**: Keeping events within budget while maintaining quality
- **Communication Overload**: Managing communication with numerous stakeholders
- **Weather Dependencies**: Outdoor events affected by unpredictable conditions
- **Last-minute Changes**: Handling unexpected changes and emergencies
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring consistent quality across different event components
- **Volunteer Management**: Coordinating large numbers of volunteers effectively

## Opportunities
- **Integrated Event Platform**: Comprehensive system managing all event aspects
- **Mobile Event Apps**: Real-time coordination and communication tools
- **Automated Workflows**: Streamlined processes for recurring event tasks
- **Digital Registration**: Online registration and participation management
- **Live Streaming**: Virtual participation and broader audience reach
- **Social Media Integration**: Real-time social media engagement and promotion
- **Analytics & Insights**: Data-driven event improvement and planning

## Inputs
- **Event Specifications**: Type, scale, objectives, target audience, success criteria
- **Resource Inventory**: Available venues, equipment, personnel, budget allocations
- **Stakeholder Requirements**: Administrative expectations, educational objectives, participant needs
- **Calendar Information**: Academic schedule, competing events, holiday considerations
- **Vendor Information**: Service provider capabilities, pricing, availability
- **Safety Requirements**: Security protocols, emergency procedures, compliance needs
- **Communication Materials**: Invitation templates, promotional content, branding guidelines

## Outputs
- **Event Plans**: Comprehensive planning documents with timelines and resource allocation
- **Coordination Schedules**: Detailed schedules for all participants and vendors
- **Logistics Arrangements**: Venue setup, transportation, catering, and equipment plans
- **Communication Materials**: Invitations, announcements, promotional content, reminders
- **Execution Procedures**: Day-of-event procedures and emergency response plans
- **Documentation**: Event photos, videos, attendance records, proceedings
- **Post-Event Reports**: Feedback analysis, financial reports, success metrics, improvement recommendations

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Integrated platform manages all event phases from planning to post-event analysis
- [ ] Resource booking prevents conflicts and optimizes utilization across multiple events
- [ ] Mobile coordination enables real-time communication with 500+ event participants
- [ ] Budget tracking provides live expense monitoring with automated approval workflows
- [ ] Digital registration handles 1000+ participants with payment processing integration
- [ ] Communication system reaches 100% of intended recipients across multiple channels
- [ ] Live documentation captures event proceedings with automatic upload and organization
- [ ] Feedback collection achieves 80%+ response rates with automated analysis
- [ ] Financial reconciliation completes within 48 hours of event conclusion
- [ ] Analytics dashboard provides comprehensive event success metrics and improvement insights

## System Interactions
- **Academic Calendar**: Coordinate event scheduling with educational activities
- **Finance Management**: Track event budgets and process vendor payments
- **Facility Management**: Book venues and coordinate setup requirements
- **Communication Platform**: Manage event communications and notifications
- **Student Information**: Access participant data and manage registrations
- **Vendor Management**: Coordinate with external service providers and contractors
- **Document Management**: Store event documentation, contracts, and media
- **Transportation System**: Coordinate participant and guest transportation
- **Safety Management**: Implement security protocols and emergency procedures

## Edge Cases
- **Weather Emergencies**: Alternative arrangements for outdoor events during severe weather
- **Medical Emergencies**: Rapid response procedures for participant health incidents
- **Security Threats**: Event modification or cancellation due to security concerns
- **Vendor Failures**: Emergency replacement of critical vendors or services
- **Technology Failures**: Backup procedures for audio-visual and technical systems
- **Overcrowding**: Safety management when attendance exceeds expected levels
- **VIP Requirements**: Special arrangements for important guests and dignitaries
- **Media Incidents**: Managing unexpected media attention or publicity issues
- **Budget Overruns**: Financial management when expenses exceed allocated budgets
- **Multi-location Events**: Coordination across multiple venues or campuses

## Priority/Frequency
- **Priority**: Medium to High (Important for school culture and community engagement)
- **Frequency**:
  - Annual events: Major cultural programs, sports days, graduation ceremonies
  - Seasonal events: Festival celebrations, competition seasons, award ceremonies
  - Monthly activities: Club events, workshops, guest lectures, exhibitions
  - Weekly programs: Assembly programs, student presentations, small-scale events
  - Ad-hoc events: Emergency assemblies, special celebrations, visiting dignitary programs
- **Peak Usage**: Cultural festival seasons, sports competition periods, academic milestone celebrations
- **Critical Periods**: Annual day preparations, inter-school competitions, board visit events
