# Event Planning Requirements

## User Story
As an event coordinator/activities director, I want to efficiently plan school events including cultural programs, sports competitions, academic competitions, and celebrations by managing dates, budgets, venues, permissions, and resource allocation so that events are successful, well-organized, and align with educational objectives.

## Actors
- **Event Coordinator** → Plans events, manages timelines, coordinates resources, oversees execution
- **Activities Director** → Sets event strategy, approves plans, manages budgets, evaluates outcomes
- **Principal** → Authorizes events, approves budgets, provides institutional support, ensures alignment
- **Department Heads** → Propose departmental events, provide subject expertise, allocate staff resources
- **Teacher Coordinators** → Lead event committees, mentor students, manage academic integration
- **Student Committee Leaders** → Organize student participation, coordinate peer activities, provide student perspective
- **Venue Providers** → Supply event spaces, provide setup services, ensure facility compliance
- **Equipment Suppliers** → Rent technical equipment, provide maintenance support, offer installation services
- **Performance Groups** → Deliver entertainment, conduct workshops, provide cultural programs
- **Government Authorities** → Issue permits, conduct safety inspections, ensure regulatory compliance

## Journey (Steps)

### 1. Event Conceptualization & Approval
- Identify event opportunities and educational objectives
- Develop event concepts and preliminary planning documents
- Seek administrative approval and budget allocation
- Establish event committees and leadership structure

### 2. Strategic Planning & Scheduling
- Create detailed event timelines and milestone schedules
- Coordinate with academic calendar and avoid scheduling conflicts
- Plan resource requirements including venues, equipment, and personnel
- Develop risk assessment and contingency planning

### 3. Budget Planning & Financial Management
- Prepare detailed event budgets with cost projections
- Secure funding sources and sponsorship opportunities
- Track expenses and manage budget allocations
- Handle financial approvals and procurement processes

### 4. Venue & Logistics Planning
- Research and book appropriate venues for different event components
- Plan seating arrangements, stage setup, and technical requirements
- Coordinate transportation and parking arrangements
- Arrange necessary permits and government approvals

### 5. Timeline & Milestone Management
- Create comprehensive project timelines with critical milestones
- Assign responsibilities and deadlines to team members
- Monitor progress and adjust plans based on developments
- Coordinate dependencies and cross-functional activities

## Pain Points
- **Resource Conflicts**: Multiple events competing for same venues, equipment, and personnel
- **Budget Constraints**: Limited funding affecting event quality and scope
- **Timeline Pressure**: Tight schedules with overlapping academic commitments
- **Approval Delays**: Slow administrative and government approval processes
- **Coordination Complexity**: Managing multiple stakeholders and dependencies
- **Weather Dependencies**: Outdoor events affected by unpredictable weather conditions
- **Permit Challenges**: Complex government permissions and compliance requirements
- **Vendor Reliability**: Dependence on external vendors and service providers

## Opportunities
- **Digital Planning Platforms**: Comprehensive event management software with integrated planning tools
- **Automated Scheduling**: Smart scheduling that avoids conflicts and optimizes resource utilization
- **Budget Tracking Tools**: Real-time budget monitoring and expense management capabilities
- **Vendor Management Systems**: Centralized vendor coordination and contract management
- **Mobile Collaboration**: Mobile apps for real-time team coordination and updates
- **Risk Management Tools**: Automated risk assessment and contingency planning features
- **Integration Platforms**: Seamless integration with academic calendar and facility booking systems

## Inputs
- **Event Requirements**: Event objectives, scope, target audience, success criteria
- **Budget Information**: Available funding, cost estimates, sponsorship opportunities
- **Calendar Constraints**: Academic schedule, facility availability, competing events
- **Resource Inventory**: Available venues, equipment, personnel, and support services
- **Regulatory Requirements**: Government permissions, safety regulations, compliance needs
- **Stakeholder Input**: Administrator expectations, teacher requirements, student preferences
- **Historical Data**: Previous event experiences, lessons learned, best practices

## Outputs
- **Event Proposals**: Comprehensive event concepts with objectives and resource requirements
- **Project Plans**: Detailed timelines, milestones, and responsibility assignments
- **Budget Documents**: Detailed cost projections and funding allocation plans
- **Venue Bookings**: Confirmed facility reservations and setup requirements
- **Permission Documentation**: Government approvals, permits, and compliance certificates
- **Resource Allocation**: Equipment, personnel, and vendor assignment schedules
- **Risk Management Plans**: Identified risks, mitigation strategies, and contingency procedures

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Event planning system prevents double-booking of venues and resources automatically
- [ ] Budget tracking provides real-time expense monitoring with automated alerts at 80% utilization
- [ ] Timeline management shows critical path analysis and dependency tracking
- [ ] Integration with academic calendar identifies and prevents scheduling conflicts
- [ ] Mobile access enables real-time planning updates and team collaboration
- [ ] Document management stores all event documentation with version control
- [ ] Approval workflows route requests to appropriate authorities with tracking
- [ ] Resource management shows availability and allocation across all planned events
- [ ] Risk assessment tools identify potential issues and suggest mitigation strategies
- [ ] Reporting dashboard provides event planning status and progress tracking

## System Interactions
- **Academic Calendar**: Check schedule conflicts and coordinate with educational activities
- **Facility Management**: Book venues and coordinate setup requirements
- **Budget Management**: Track event expenses and coordinate with financial systems
- **Vendor Management**: Coordinate with external suppliers and service providers
- **Communication Platform**: Coordinate team communications and stakeholder updates
- **Document Management**: Store event plans, contracts, and approval documentation
- **Staff Management**: Assign personnel and coordinate staff availability
- **Student Activities**: Integrate with student club and activity coordination
- **Safety Management**: Coordinate safety requirements and emergency planning

## Edge Cases
- **Weather Emergencies**: Alternative planning for outdoor events during adverse weather
- **Venue Cancellations**: Rapid reallocation when booked venues become unavailable
- **Budget Cuts**: Scaling down events when funding is reduced or eliminated
- **Permit Denials**: Alternative approaches when government permissions are declined
- **Key Personnel Unavailability**: Backup planning when essential staff are unavailable
- **Vendor Failures**: Alternative arrangements when contracted vendors cannot deliver
- **Emergency Situations**: Event cancellation and communication during crisis situations
- **Technology Failures**: Manual coordination methods when planning systems fail
- **Scheduling Conflicts**: Last-minute rescheduling when conflicts arise
- **Capacity Changes**: Adapting events when venue capacity or safety requirements change

## Priority/Frequency
- **Priority**: Medium to High (Important for school culture and student development)
- **Frequency**:
  - Annual planning: Major event calendar development and strategic planning
  - Quarterly coordination: Seasonal event planning and resource allocation
  - Monthly planning: Detailed event preparation and vendor coordination
  - Weekly monitoring: Progress tracking and timeline management
  - Event-specific intensive planning: Concentrated planning periods before major events
- **Peak Usage**: Pre-event planning periods, festival seasons, competition preparation
- **Critical Periods**: Annual event calendar preparation, major cultural events, sports competitions
