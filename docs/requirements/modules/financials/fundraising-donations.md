# Fundraising & Donations Requirements

## User Story
As a development officer/principal, I want to efficiently manage fundraising campaigns, track donations and endowments, maintain donor relationships, and ensure transparent utilization of funds so that the school can raise additional resources for infrastructure development, scholarships, and educational enhancement while maintaining donor trust and compliance.

## Actors
- **Primary**: Development Officer, Principal, Finance Manager
- **Secondary**: Marketing Team, Alumni Coordinator, Board Members
- **External**: Donors (Individual/Corporate), Alumni, Foundation Partners, Grant Agencies

## Journey (Steps)

### 1. Fundraising Campaign Planning
- Define fundraising goals and target amounts
- Create campaign strategies and timelines
- Identify potential donor segments and prospects
- Set up campaign tracking and monitoring systems

### 2. Donor Outreach & Engagement
- Launch marketing campaigns across multiple channels
- Organize fundraising events and donor meetings
- Create personalized donor communication strategies
- Maintain regular donor engagement and updates

### 3. Donation Collection & Processing
- Accept donations through multiple channels (online, offline, events)
- Process different donation types (cash, cheque, online, securities, assets)
- Generate tax-compliant donation receipts
- Handle recurring donations and pledges

### 4. Donor Relationship Management
- Maintain comprehensive donor database with interaction history
- Track donor preferences and giving patterns
- Organize donor recognition events and awards
- Provide regular updates on fund utilization and impact

### 5. Fund Management & Reporting
- Allocate funds to designated purposes and projects
- Track fund utilization against donor intent
- Generate impact reports and transparency statements
- Prepare regulatory compliance and tax filings

## Pain Points
- **Manual Donor Tracking**: Spreadsheet-based donor management leading to missed opportunities
- **Limited Payment Options**: Restricted donation channels reducing giving convenience
- **Poor Donor Communication**: Irregular updates leading to donor dissatisfaction
- **Compliance Complexity**: Tax regulations and reporting requirements for donations
- **Impact Measurement**: Difficulty quantifying and communicating donation impact
- **Event Management**: Complex logistics for fundraising events and donor meetings
- **Data Integration**: Disconnected systems for campaigns, donations, and financial tracking
- **Recognition Management**: Manual tracking of donor acknowledgments and recognition

## Opportunities
- **Digital Fundraising Platform**: Online donation portal with multiple payment options
- **CRM Integration**: Comprehensive donor relationship management system
- **Automated Campaigns**: Email marketing automation for donor engagement
- **Social Media Integration**: Crowdfunding and social media donation campaigns
- **Impact Analytics**: Real-time tracking and reporting of donation impact
- **Mobile Giving**: Mobile app for easy donation and donor engagement
- **Grant Management**: Systematic tracking of grant applications and compliance

## Inputs
- **Donor Information**: Personal/corporate details, contact information, giving history
- **Campaign Data**: Fundraising goals, timelines, target segments, marketing materials
- **Donation Transactions**: Amount, method, date, purpose, donor instructions
- **Event Information**: Fundraising event details, attendance, costs, collections
- **Project Requirements**: Infrastructure needs, scholarship funds, equipment requirements
- **Regulatory Guidelines**: Tax laws, compliance requirements, reporting formats
- **Communication Preferences**: Donor preferences for communication and recognition

## Outputs
- **Donation Receipts**: Tax-compliant receipts with proper legal formatting
- **Campaign Reports**: Fundraising performance against targets and timelines
- **Donor Statements**: Annual giving statements and tax documentation
- **Impact Reports**: Project completion and fund utilization reports
- **Thank You Communications**: Acknowledgment letters and appreciation certificates
- **Compliance Filings**: Regulatory returns and transparency reports
- **Prospect Lists**: Potential donor identification and scoring reports

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Online donation platform processes payments with 99.9% uptime
- [ ] Donation receipts generate automatically within 24 hours of payment
- [ ] Campaign tracking provides real-time progress against targets
- [ ] Donor database maintains complete interaction history and preferences
- [ ] Fund allocation tracking shows 100% transparency in utilization
- [ ] Tax compliance reports generate automatically for regulatory filing
- [ ] Recognition system tracks and schedules all donor acknowledgments
- [ ] Impact measurement provides quantifiable outcomes for donor reporting
- [ ] Grant application tracking manages deadlines and compliance requirements
- [ ] Mobile platform enables donations from any device with single-click convenience

## System Interactions
- **Fee Management**: Ensure donations don't conflict with fee collection processes
- **Accounting System**: Post donation entries to appropriate fund accounts
- **Banking System**: Process donation payments and reconcile receipts
- **Communication Platform**: Send automated acknowledgments and updates
- **Event Management**: Coordinate fundraising events and track collections
- **Student Information**: Identify scholarship recipients and impact beneficiaries
- **Project Management**: Track fund utilization for specific projects and initiatives
- **Tax Management**: Generate tax receipts and compliance reports
- **Document Management**: Store donor agreements, receipts, and correspondence

## Edge Cases
- **Anonymous Donations**: Handling donors who wish to remain anonymous
- **Conditional Donations**: Managing donations with specific usage conditions
- **International Donors**: Processing foreign donations with currency conversion
- **Legacy Donations**: Managing bequests and estate donations
- **Corporate Partnerships**: Handling complex corporate sponsorship agreements
- **Cancelled Donations**: Reversing donations and refund processing
- **Disputed Transactions**: Handling payment disputes and chargebacks
- **Endowment Management**: Long-term investment and utilization of endowment funds
- **Emergency Fundraising**: Rapid response campaigns for urgent needs
- **Regulatory Changes**: Adapting to changes in donation tax laws and compliance

## Priority/Frequency
- **Priority**: Medium to High (Important for school development and growth)
- **Frequency**:
  - Campaign management: Seasonal campaigns, annual giving drives
  - Donation processing: Daily during active campaigns
  - Donor communication: Monthly newsletters, quarterly impact reports
  - Event management: 2-4 major fundraising events per year
  - Compliance reporting: Annual filings, quarterly reviews
- **Peak Usage**: Annual giving seasons, major event periods, capital campaign phases
- **Critical Periods**: Year-end giving season, alumni homecoming, capital campaign launches
