# Recruitment & Onboarding Requirements

## User Story
As an HR manager/principal, I want to efficiently manage the complete teacher and staff recruitment process using digital platforms and automated workflows so that we attract qualified candidates, conduct fair selection processes, and ensure smooth integration of new employees into the school community.

## Actors
- **HR Manager** → Manages recruitment process, coordinates interviews, handles candidate communication
- **Principal** → Approves hiring decisions, defines job requirements, participates in final interviews
- **Department Heads** → Define role specifications, conduct technical interviews, provide hiring recommendations
- **Job Applicants** → Submit applications, attend interviews, provide documentation, complete onboarding
- **Interview Panel Members** → Evaluate candidates, provide feedback, participate in selection decisions
- **Background Verification Agencies** → Verify candidate credentials, conduct reference checks, provide verification reports

## Journey (Steps)

### 1. Job Planning & Posting
- Identify staffing needs and create detailed job descriptions
- Define qualification requirements and selection criteria
- Post job openings on multiple platforms and channels
- Manage recruitment budgets and agency relationships

### 2. Application Management & Screening
- Collect and organize applications from various sources
- Conduct initial screening based on qualifications and experience
- Schedule and coordinate preliminary interviews
- Manage candidate communication and status updates

### 3. Interview & Assessment Process
- Organize interview panels and assessment criteria
- Conduct multiple rounds of interviews and evaluations
- Verify references and conduct background checks
- Make selection decisions and prepare offer letters

### 4. Offer Management & Negotiation
- Prepare salary offers and benefits packages
- Handle salary negotiations and contract terms
- Process joining formalities and documentation
- Coordinate with finance for salary setup and benefits

### 5. Onboarding & Integration
- Plan orientation programs and welcome activities
- Complete documentation and compliance requirements
- Provide initial training and mentorship assignments
- Monitor probation period and performance evaluation

## Pain Points
- **Manual Application Tracking**: Paper-based or scattered digital applications difficult to manage
- **Interview Coordination**: Complex scheduling with multiple stakeholders and candidates
- **Document Verification**: Time-consuming background checks and reference verification
- **Communication Gaps**: Poor candidate experience due to delayed or unclear communication
- **Compliance Issues**: Incomplete documentation affecting legal and regulatory compliance
- **Onboarding Delays**: Slow onboarding process affecting new employee productivity
- **Data Management**: Scattered candidate and employee data across multiple systems
- **Cost Control**: Difficulty tracking recruitment costs and ROI of different channels

## Opportunities
- **Digital Recruitment Platform**: End-to-end online recruitment and application management
- **AI-powered Screening**: Automated initial screening based on qualifications and fit
- **Video Interview Integration**: Remote interview capabilities and recorded assessments
- **Background Verification APIs**: Automated verification of documents and references
- **Mobile Recruitment**: Mobile-friendly application process and communication
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Data-driven insights on recruitment effectiveness and costs
- **Integration Hub**: Seamless transition from recruitment to HR and payroll systems

## Inputs
- **Job Requirements**: Position details, qualifications, experience requirements, salary ranges
- **Application Data**: Resumes, cover letters, certificates, portfolio submissions
- **Interview Feedback**: Panel evaluations, assessment scores, behavioral observations
- **Reference Information**: Previous employer feedback, character references, recommendations
- **Background Verification**: Document authenticity, criminal checks, educational verification
- **Offer Details**: Salary negotiations, benefits, joining dates, contract terms
- **Compliance Documents**: Legal requirements, tax forms, insurance paperwork

## Outputs
- **Job Postings**: Professional job advertisements for multiple platforms
- **Candidate Profiles**: Comprehensive applicant information and assessment summaries
- **Interview Reports**: Detailed evaluation reports and selection recommendations
- **Offer Letters**: Official employment offers with terms and conditions
- **Employment Contracts**: Legal agreements with salary and benefit details
- **Onboarding Plans**: Structured orientation and training schedules
- **Analytics Reports**: Recruitment metrics, cost analysis, and effectiveness measures

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Application tracking system manages 500+ applications simultaneously without performance issues
- [ ] Automated screening filters candidates based on predefined criteria with 90%+ accuracy
- [ ] Interview scheduling coordinates with all panel members and sends automated confirmations
- [ ] Background verification completes within 5 business days with digital documentation
- [ ] Offer generation includes accurate salary calculations and benefits information
- [ ] Onboarding portal guides new employees through all required documentation and training
- [ ] Integration ensures seamless data transfer to HR and payroll systems
- [ ] Mobile application provides candidate-friendly experience across all recruitment stages
- [ ] Analytics dashboard shows real-time recruitment metrics and cost analysis
- [ ] Compliance tracking ensures 100% completion of required documentation

## System Interactions
- **Job Portal APIs**: Post openings to external job sites and recruitment platforms
- **Email/SMS Service**: Automated communication with candidates throughout the process
- **Document Management**: Store resumes, certificates, and recruitment documentation
- **Calendar Integration**: Schedule interviews and coordinate with multiple stakeholders
- **Payroll System**: Set up new employee salary and benefits information
- **Employee Management**: Create employee profiles and access permissions
- **Background Verification**: Interface with external verification agencies and services
- **Analytics Engine**: Track recruitment metrics and generate performance reports
- **Compliance System**: Ensure all legal and regulatory requirements are met

## Edge Cases
- **Emergency Hiring**: Rapid recruitment for critical positions with accelerated processes
- **Internal Transfers**: Handling internal candidate applications and promotions
- **Bulk Recruitment**: Mass hiring for new academic year or expansion
- **Candidate Withdrawal**: Managing late-stage candidate dropouts and backup selections
- **Reference Unavailability**: Alternative verification methods when references are unreachable
- **Document Discrepancies**: Handling fraudulent or mismatched documentation
- **Visa Requirements**: International candidate hiring with work permit processing
- **Seasonal Positions**: Temporary and part-time recruitment with different workflows
- **Re-hiring**: Processing applications from former employees
- **Contract Negotiations**: Complex salary and benefit negotiations requiring approval workflows

## Priority/Frequency
- **Priority**: High (Critical for maintaining adequate staffing and school quality)
- **Frequency**:
  - Annual planning: Major recruitment drives before academic year start
  - Quarterly hiring: Replacement hiring and expansion positions
  - Emergency recruitment: As needed for unexpected departures
  - Onboarding activities: Monthly new employee integration processes
  - Performance reviews: Probation evaluations and confirmation processes
- **Peak Usage**: Pre-academic year (April-June), replacement hiring periods
- **Critical Periods**: Academic year preparation, mid-year departures, expansion phases
