# Hostel, Canteen & Library Management Requirements

## User Story
As a facilities manager/administrator, I want to efficiently manage hostel accommodations, canteen operations, and library services using integrated digital systems so that students have quality support services while maintaining operational efficiency, cost control, and real-time service tracking.

## Actors
- **Facilities Manager** → Oversees all facility operations, manages budgets, coordinates service quality
- **Hostel Warden** → Manages room allocations, student welfare, discipline, emergency procedures
- **Canteen Manager** → Plans menus, manages food service, handles payments, ensures food safety
- **Librarian** → Manages book catalog, handles book issues/returns, maintains digital resources
- **Students** → Book facilities, make payments, access services, provide feedback
- **Parents** → Pay facility fees, monitor service usage, receive service notifications
- **Food Vendors** → Supply raw materials, coordinate with canteen operations, maintain quality standards

## Journey (Steps)

### 1. Facility Setup & Resource Management
- Configure hostel rooms, canteen seating, and library sections
- Maintain inventory of books, food items, and hostel amenities
- Set up access controls and usage policies for each facility
- Define pricing structures and service packages

### 2. Student Registration & Access Control
- Register students for hostel accommodation and meal plans
- Issue library cards and set borrowing privileges
- Configure access permissions and facility usage rights
- Collect deposits and process service fees

### 3. Daily Operations & Service Delivery
- Manage hostel check-ins, room assignments, and daily maintenance
- Coordinate meal preparation, serving, and quality control
- Handle book borrowing, returns, and reservation systems
- Monitor facility utilization and service quality

### 4. Billing & Financial Management
- Generate monthly bills for hostel and canteen services
- Track library fines, lost book charges, and damage fees
- Process payments and manage outstanding dues
- Provide cost analysis and budgeting support

### 5. Monitoring & Quality Assurance
- Conduct regular facility inspections and maintenance
- Monitor food quality, safety, and student satisfaction
- Track library usage patterns and resource effectiveness
- Generate utilization reports and improvement recommendations

## Pain Points
- **Manual Coordination**: Complex coordination between multiple facility operations
- **Inventory Management**: Difficulty tracking books, food stock, and hostel supplies
- **Access Control**: Manual processes for facility access and usage tracking
- **Billing Complexity**: Multiple billing cycles and payment tracking for different services
- **Quality Monitoring**: Inconsistent service quality and maintenance standards
- **Resource Utilization**: Suboptimal use of facilities and resources
- **Student Satisfaction**: Limited feedback mechanisms and service improvement
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Food safety, library standards, and hostel regulations

## Opportunities
- **Integrated Platform**: Unified management system for all facility operations
- **Mobile Access**: Student mobile apps for booking, payments, and service requests
- **RFID Integration**: Automated access control and inventory tracking
- **Digital Menus**: Dynamic meal planning and nutritional information
- **Smart Analytics**: Data-driven insights for resource optimization and planning
- **Quality Monitoring**: Automated feedback collection and service quality tracking
- **Predictive Maintenance**: Proactive facility maintenance and resource planning

## Inputs
- **Student Information**: Enrollment details, accommodation preferences, dietary requirements
- **Facility Data**: Room capacity, equipment inventory, service area layouts
- **Menu Planning**: Meal options, nutritional requirements, dietary restrictions
- **Book Catalog**: Library collection, categories, availability status
- **Pricing Information**: Service charges, meal plans, hostel fees, library fines
- **Vendor Details**: Food suppliers, book publishers, maintenance contractors
- **Usage Policies**: Facility rules, borrowing limits, access restrictions

## Outputs
- **Accommodation Assignments**: Hostel room allocations and roommate assignments
- **Meal Plans**: Individual and group meal scheduling and menu distribution
- **Library Catalogues**: Digital book catalogs with search and reservation capabilities
- **Billing Statements**: Monthly service charges and payment due notifications
- **Utilization Reports**: Facility usage statistics and optimization recommendations
- **Quality Reports**: Service quality assessments and improvement plans
- **Inventory Reports**: Stock levels, procurement needs, and cost analysis

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Hostel management handles room booking and assignment within 24 hours
- [ ] Canteen system processes meal orders and payments in real-time
- [ ] Library catalog search returns results within 3 seconds with 99%+ accuracy
- [ ] Access control system grants/denies facility entry within 2 seconds
- [ ] Billing integration generates accurate monthly statements for all services
- [ ] Mobile app enables students to access all facility services from single platform
- [ ] Inventory tracking maintains real-time stock levels with automatic reorder alerts
- [ ] Quality monitoring collects and analyzes student feedback automatically
- [ ] Integration ensures seamless data flow with student information and fee systems
- [ ] Reporting dashboard provides real-time facility utilization and financial metrics

## System Interactions
- **Student Information System**: Access student enrollment and accommodation eligibility
- **Fee Management**: Integrate facility charges with overall fee collection
- **Access Control System**: Manage student access to hostel, canteen, and library
- **Inventory Management**: Track books, food items, and facility supplies
- **Payment Gateway**: Process online payments for services and deposits
- **Communication Platform**: Send notifications about bookings, dues, and announcements
- **Maintenance System**: Schedule and track facility repairs and upkeep
- **Analytics Engine**: Generate utilization reports and optimization insights
- **Mobile Application**: Student access to facility services and information

## Edge Cases
- **Emergency Situations**: Evacuation procedures and emergency accommodation
- **Dietary Restrictions**: Special meal arrangements for medical or religious requirements
- **Lost/Damaged Items**: Processing library book replacements and hostel damage charges
- **Facility Maintenance**: Service disruptions during repairs and alternative arrangements
- **Seasonal Variations**: Adjusted operations during holidays and exam periods
- **Capacity Overflow**: Waiting lists and alternative accommodation during peak demand
- **Payment Delays**: Service suspension and restoration procedures for outstanding dues
- **Technology Failures**: Manual backup procedures for access control and booking systems
- **Health Emergencies**: Quarantine procedures and meal delivery arrangements
- **Special Events**: Modified facility operations during school events and programs

## Priority/Frequency
- **Priority**: Medium to High (Important for residential school operations and student satisfaction)
- **Frequency**:
  - Daily operations: Meal service, room maintenance, library access monitoring
  - Weekly planning: Menu updates, inventory checks, facility inspections
  - Monthly billing: Service charges, accommodation fees, library fine processing
  - Quarterly reviews: Service quality assessment, cost optimization, policy updates
  - Annual planning: Facility upgrades, contract renewals, budget preparation
- **Peak Usage**: Hostel admission periods, exam times (library), meal hours (canteen)
- **Critical Periods**: Academic year start (room allocation), inventory restocking, inspection periods
