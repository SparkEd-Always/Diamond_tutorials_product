# Safety, Health & Security Requirements

## User Story
As a safety officer/school administrator, I want to maintain comprehensive safety, health, and security protocols using automated monitoring and alert systems so that students and staff are protected from risks while creating a secure learning environment that complies with regulatory standards.

## Actors
- **Safety Officer** → Develops safety protocols, conducts risk assessments, coordinates emergency response
- **Security Supervisor** → Manages security operations, monitors CCTV, coordinates with external security
- **School Nurse** → Handles medical emergencies, maintains health records, conducts health screenings
- **Principal** → Approves safety policies, coordinates with authorities, manages crisis communication
- **Students & Staff** → Follow safety protocols, report incidents, participate in emergency drills
- **Emergency Services** → Respond to critical incidents, provide training, conduct safety inspections

## Journey (Steps)

### 1. Safety Protocol Setup & Planning
- Establish comprehensive safety policies and emergency procedures
- Install and maintain safety equipment (fire extinguishers, alarms, CCTV)
- Conduct risk assessments and vulnerability analysis
- Create evacuation plans and emergency response procedures

### 2. Health Monitoring & Medical Care
- Conduct regular health screenings and medical checkups
- Maintain student health records and medical histories
- Manage first aid stations and medical emergency responses
- Coordinate with healthcare providers and parents for medical needs

### 3. Security Operations & Access Control
- Monitor CCTV systems and maintain surveillance coverage
- Manage visitor access and identification procedures
- Coordinate security guard schedules and patrol routes
- Control entry/exit points and maintain security logs

### 4. Emergency Response & Crisis Management
- Implement emergency response procedures for various scenarios
- Coordinate with emergency services and law enforcement
- Conduct regular safety drills and training sessions
- Manage crisis communication and parent notifications

### 5. Compliance & Documentation
- Ensure compliance with safety and health regulations
- Maintain incident reports and investigation records
- Conduct regular safety audits and inspections
- Generate compliance reports for regulatory authorities

## Pain Points
- **Manual Monitoring**: Inadequate surveillance and manual security processes
- **Slow Emergency Response**: Delayed response during medical or security emergencies
- **Incomplete Documentation**: Poor incident reporting and compliance record keeping
- **Communication Gaps**: Ineffective communication during emergencies and crises
- **Training Deficiencies**: Inadequate safety training for staff and students
- **Equipment Maintenance**: Poor maintenance of safety and security equipment
- **Visitor Management**: Inefficient visitor tracking and identification processes
- **Medical Record Management**: Scattered health information affecting emergency response

## Opportunities
- **Smart Security Systems**: AI-powered surveillance with automatic threat detection
- **Emergency Alert Systems**: Instant notification systems for rapid emergency response
- **Health Management Platform**: Comprehensive digital health records and monitoring
- **Mobile Safety Apps**: Mobile applications for emergency reporting and communication
- **IoT Safety Monitoring**: Smart sensors for fire, air quality, and environmental monitoring
- **Automated Compliance**: Digital compliance tracking and audit preparation
- **Visitor Management System**: Digital visitor registration and tracking platform

## Inputs
- **Safety Policies**: Emergency procedures, safety protocols, regulatory requirements
- **Health Information**: Student medical records, allergies, medications, emergency contacts
- **Security Infrastructure**: CCTV systems, access control points, alarm systems
- **Personnel Data**: Security staff schedules, trained first aid responders, emergency contacts
- **Incident Reports**: Safety incidents, medical emergencies, security breaches
- **Equipment Inventory**: Safety equipment, medical supplies, security devices
- **Emergency Contacts**: Local emergency services, hospitals, law enforcement, parents

## Outputs
- **Safety Reports**: Incident reports, safety audit results, compliance documentation
- **Health Records**: Student health profiles, medical emergency procedures, vaccination records
- **Security Logs**: Access logs, surveillance reports, visitor tracking records
- **Emergency Procedures**: Crisis response plans, evacuation procedures, contact protocols
- **Training Records**: Safety drill reports, staff training certificates, competency assessments
- **Compliance Certificates**: Regulatory compliance documentation and audit reports
- **Emergency Alerts**: Instant notifications for security, medical, and safety emergencies

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] CCTV monitoring system provides 24/7 surveillance with 99%+ uptime
- [ ] Emergency alert system reaches all stakeholders within 3 minutes of activation
- [ ] Medical emergency response initiates within 5 minutes of incident reporting
- [ ] Visitor management system tracks 100% of campus visitors with digital records
- [ ] Safety drill evacuation completes within regulatory time limits with full accountability
- [ ] Health screening system maintains up-to-date medical records for all students
- [ ] Incident reporting system captures and categorizes all safety and security events
- [ ] Access control system manages entry/exit permissions with real-time monitoring
- [ ] Compliance tracking ensures 100% current certifications and regulatory requirements
- [ ] Mobile safety app enables instant emergency reporting from any campus location

## System Interactions
- **Student Information System**: Access student emergency contacts and medical information
- **Communication Platform**: Send emergency alerts and safety notifications
- **Access Control System**: Manage campus entry/exit and visitor permissions
- **Mobile Application**: Enable emergency reporting and safety communication
- **Document Management**: Store safety records, medical files, and compliance documentation
- **Maintenance System**: Coordinate safety equipment maintenance and inspections
- **HR Management**: Track staff safety training and certification status
- **External Systems**: Interface with emergency services and healthcare providers
- **Analytics Engine**: Analyze safety trends and risk assessment data

## Edge Cases
- **Natural Disasters**: Emergency response for earthquakes, floods, storms, and fires
- **Medical Emergencies**: Severe allergic reactions, injuries, and health crises
- **Security Threats**: Intruder alerts, violence threats, and lockdown procedures
- **Technology Failures**: Backup procedures when security and communication systems fail
- **Mass Evacuation**: Large-scale evacuations with student accountability and parent reunification
- **Infectious Disease Outbreaks**: Quarantine procedures and health monitoring protocols
- **Chemical/Biological Hazards**: Laboratory accidents and hazardous material incidents
- **Transportation Emergencies**: Bus accidents and field trip emergency procedures
- **Mental Health Crises**: Suicide threats and psychological emergency interventions
- **External Threats**: Terrorism alerts and external security threat responses

## Priority/Frequency
- **Priority**: Critical (Essential for student and staff safety and legal compliance)
- **Frequency**:
  - Continuous monitoring: 24/7 security surveillance and emergency preparedness
  - Daily operations: Health checks, visitor management, safety inspections
  - Weekly activities: Safety equipment checks, drill planning, staff training
  - Monthly reviews: Incident analysis, compliance verification, procedure updates
  - Quarterly assessments: Comprehensive safety audits, emergency drill evaluations
- **Peak Usage**: Emergency situations, large events, inspection periods
- **Critical Periods**: Emergency incidents, natural disasters, regulatory inspections, health crises
